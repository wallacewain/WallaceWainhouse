Planet Hopper

One button. Your rocket always points away from the nearest planet — hold to boost and push off it. Whip around worlds to sling toward the glowing target, then coast in (you can't boost straight at it). Line up the slingshot and hop as far as you can — it gets harder every jump.

Planet Hopper

The ship points away from the nearest planet. Hold anywhere (or Space) to boost off it — swing around planets to sling toward the blue target, then release and coast in. The dashed line shows where you'll drift. Don't smash into the red ones.

🟢 Launch pad (safe) 🔵 Target (reach it) 🔴 Hazard (avoid) ⤳ dashed line = your path if you stop boosting
